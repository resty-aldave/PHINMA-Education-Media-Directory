SouthWestern University

Southwestern University PHINMA was established in 1946 in the heart of Cebu City and has since evolved into a premier destination for higher learning that joined the prestigious PHINMA Education network in 2015. True to its core mission of "Making Lives Better through Education," the institution continues to uphold its storied legacy of academic rigor and clinical expertise, specifically within its world-class programs in medicine, dentistry, and the health sciences.

The university fosters a vibrant, inclusive, and highly supportive campus environment designed to nurture the holistic growth of every student who walks through its gates. By integrating modern practical skills with a strong foundation in ethical values, SWU PHINMA ensures that its graduates are not only ready for the local workforce but are also positioned as globally competitive leaders in their respective professional fields.
